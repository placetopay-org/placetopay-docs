# Types of Business Identification

| Identification Type | Description of the Value                               | Format                                                                                                             | Example            |
|---------------------|--------------------------------------------------------|--------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------|--------------------|
| ABN                 | Australian Business Number                             | 11 numeric digits                                                                                                  | 12345678901        |
| BID                 | Business Identification Number; all countries          | 8 numeric digits                                                                                                   | 12345678           |
| BN                  | Canadian Business Number                               | 9 numeric digits                                                                                                   | 123456789          |
| BRNO                | Only Malaysia                                          | Up to 10 numeric digits followed by a hyphen and a single character, or 12 numeric digits                          | 123456789012-1     |
| CL                  | Qatar Business License Number                          | 4–6 numeric digits                                                                                                 | 12345              |
| CLN                 | Kuwait Business License Number                         | 5–7 numeric digits                                                                                                 | 1234567            |
| CNPJ                | Only Brazil                                            | 14 alphanumeric characters, identified by "n" in the example, and punctuation characters (dot, slash, and hyphen). | nn.nnn.nnn/nnnn-nn |
| CR                  | Saudi Arabia Business Registration Number              | 10–12 numeric digits                                                                                               | 1234567890         |
| CUIT                | Individual Entrepreneurs in Ukraine                    | 10 numeric digits                                                                                                  | 1234567890         |
| EIN                 | U.S. Federal Tax ID (only U.S.)                        | 9 numeric digits                                                                                                   | 123456789          |
| HKBR                | Only Hong Kong                                         | 11 numeric digits and a hyphen                                                                                     | 12345678-123       |
| NATIONAL_ID         | Dominican Republic Identity Card (Business Owner's ID) | DO + 11 digits                                                                                                     | DO01234567890      |
| NZBN                | New Zealand Business Number                            | 13 numeric digits                                                                                                  | 1234567890123      |
| PAN                 | India Business Identification Number                   | 10 alphanumeric characters                                                                                         | abc1234567         |
| RFC                 | Only Mexico                                            | 12 alphanumeric characters for corporations; 13 alphanumeric characters for individuals                            | abc1234567890      |
| RNC                 | Dominican Republic (National Taxpayer Number)          | DO + 9 digits                                                                                                      | DO123456789        |
| RUC                 | Only Peru                                              | 11 numeric digits                                                                                                  | 12345678901        |
| RUT                 | Only Chile and Colombia                                | 9 numeric digits for Chile; 10 numeric digits for Colombia                                                         | 1234567890         |
| SIN                 | Canadian Social Insurance Number                       | 9 numeric digits                                                                                                   | 123456789          |
| SSN                 | U.S. Social Security Number (only U.S.)                | 9 numeric digits                                                                                                   | 123456789          |
| UEN                 | Only Singapore                                         | 9 or 10 alphanumeric characters                                                                                    | 123456789          |
