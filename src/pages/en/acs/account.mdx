# Account Management

To access the user account management module, click the dropdown menu located next to the user avatar (with your initials) in the top-right corner of the application.

The menu may contain the following sections:

## Account

This section displays the username and email address when selected.

- To update account information, click on the *Manage your account* option, located at the bottom of the user avatar.

- To view profile details, click the profile name located on the right side of the screen.

## Profiles

If you have multiple profiles, expanding the menu will list them all, and by clicking on a specific profile, you can switch from the profile you previously logged in with to the selected profile.

## Session

In this section, you will find the Log out button, which allows you to exit the application.
