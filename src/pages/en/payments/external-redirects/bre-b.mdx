Bre-B is Colombia's new instant and interoperable payment system, promoted by the Banco de la República. It enables instant money transfers and payments (24/7) between different banks and digital wallets, using a "key" (mobile number, ID or email) instead of sharing long account numbers.

## Benefits of Bre-B

- **Instant payments 24/7**: Users can authorize purchases in seconds, every day of the year, without waiting.
- **Total interoperability**: Works between different banks, platforms and digital wallets (Nequi, Daviplata, etc.).
- **Enhanced security**: By not sharing the account number, transaction security is increased.
- **Accessibility for users without cards**: Users can make payments directly from their bank accounts or digital wallets.

## Use cases

- **Frictionless instant payments**: Ideal for merchants who want to offer a digital payment method via QR code, allowing users to complete their purchase quickly from their bank accounts or digital wallets.
- **Broader reach for customers with bank access**: By enabling direct payments from bank accounts and digital wallets, Bre-B becomes an attractive alternative for merchants whose audience prefers not to use credit cards.

## Payment Method Behavior

Bre-B is a redirected payment method that operates through a QR code flow. The user completes a form with their personal information, the system generates a unique QR code that must be scanned from their banking application or digital wallet to securely authorize the transaction.

![Bre-B payment flow](/en/payments/breb/pm-breb-flow.svg)

### Bre-B Payment Flow:

1. The user completes the payment form with their information: email, identification, first name, last name, and phone number.
2. The device fingerprint is captured for future security validations.
3. Webcheckout requests QR code generation through REST.
4. The system presents the QR code for the user to scan from their banking application or digital wallet.
5. The user authorizes the transaction in their banking application.
6. The gateway performs polling every minute to verify the transaction status.
7. Once authorized, the final transaction status is displayed to the user.

- **Integrations through**: [Webcheckout](/en/checkout)
   Once the user completes the payment form, a QR code is automatically generated that must be scanned from their banking application or digital wallet. The user authorizes the transaction in their app, and the gateway verifies the status every minute until the transaction is confirmed. After authorizing the payment, the user will see the final transaction status on the gateway.

- **Integrations through**: [Gateway](/en/gateway)
   Integrations are asynchronous: the QR code is delivered as a text payload (string), not as an image or base64; the integrator decides how to render and display it to the user.

   To obtain the final transaction status, a request must be made to the [Query transaction by internal reference](/en/gateway/api/reference/transaction#query-request) endpoint.

## Restrictions and Considerations

- **Transaction limit**: The system allows movements of up to COP 1,000 UVB per transaction.
- **Not tokenizable**: Bre-B does not support tokenization of payment data for future transactions.
- **Not available in PlacetoPay wallet**: This payment method cannot be saved in the PlacetoPay wallet.
- **No support for fund dispersion**: Bre-B is not supported in fund dispersion operations.
- **Multiple providers**: The system contemplates the existence of multiple Bre-B providers, providing greater flexibility and redundancy.
- **Partial payments allowed**: The payment method can be used for partial payments.
