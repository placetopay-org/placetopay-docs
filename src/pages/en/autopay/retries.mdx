# Retries in AutoPay

The AutoPay system has an automatic retry mechanism designed to ensure the continuity of collections in the event of
temporary connection failures or unavailability of external services. The workflow includes four key points that utilize
this automatic retry mechanism, which are detailed below.

## Generation

In this phase, the system identifies that a charge needs to be made and prepares the necessary information for the
AutoPay collection.

### Balance Inquiry
The system determines whether to check the amount to be charged based on the charge type configured in AutoPay
([See Charge Types Details](/autopay/charge-types)). The charges that require consultation are:
* Total Balance (`TOTAL_BALANCE`): The total outstanding balance for the current period is checked with the merchant's
service.
* Minimum Balance (`MINIMUM_BALANCE`): The minimum balance required to maintain active service is checked, according to
the merchant's report.

### Retry Frequency
If an error occurs while attempting to check the balance or register the payment, the system will attempt up to 3
re-attempts to register the payment at the following intervals:
* **First re-attempt:** after 5 minutes.
* **Second re-attempt:** after 10 minutes.
* **Third re-attempt:** after 20 minutes.

### Attempt Limit
If the payment cannot be registered after 3 re-attempts, the system will give up, and the Self-Pay service will be
marked as failed (FAILED).
Note: If this process fails, not only is the current payment affected, but the status of all pending installments for
that AutoPayment is also updated to failed.

## Processing
Once the payment is generated with the corresponding amount, the transaction is processed against the payment method.

### Retry Frequency
In the event of processable rejections or technical communication errors with the payment gateway, the system applies
the following retry intervals:
* **First retry:** after 1 minute.
* **Second retry:** after 3 minutes.
* **Third retry:** after 5 minutes.

### Amount Consistency
During these retries, **the balance is not checked again**. This is a process performed during the installment
registration stage to ensure consistency in the amount charged.

### Attempt Limit
The total number of attempts is defined by the `max_retries` parameter, which currently has a default value of 3
attempts (not configurable at this time).
If this limit is reached without approval:
* The associated payment and the main Self-Checkout change to a failed (FAILED) status.
* The recurrence cycle stops.

## Settlement
This process is executed only when a Self-Checkout payment has been approved. Its purpose is to inform the merchant's
external service that the payment has been successfully completed.

### Retry Frequency
If the merchant service does not respond or fails to confirm the transaction, retries are made at the following time
intervals:
* **First retry:** after 10 minutes
* **Second retry:** after 20 minutes
* **Third retry:** after 30 minutes

### Transaction Failure
When the 3 attempts to consume the external service are exhausted, the Self-Checkout system logs a warning for manual
auditing.

## Notification
This flow informs the merchant system about the outcome of different events related to a Self-Checkout transaction.

### Retry Frequency
If the merchant server does not return a satisfactory response, retries are made at the following time intervals:
* **First retry:** after 1 minute.
* **Second retry:** after 5 minutes.
* **Third retry:** after 10 minutes.

### Delivery Failure
After exhausting 3 notification attempts, the system stops automatic sending and generates an error log detailing the
unreported event.

## Flow Table

| Stage            | Intervals (Backoff) - (minutes) | Attempt Limit | Check Balance? | Action on Attempt Exhaustion    |
|------------------|---------------------------------|---------------|----------------|---------------------------------|
| **Generation**   | 5, 10, and 20                   | 3             | Yes            | Subscription Failed             |
| **Processing**   | 1, 3, and 5                     | 3             | No             | Billing and Subscription Failed |
| **Settlement**   | 10, 20, and 30                  | 3             | Yes            | Warning Log (Audit)             |
| **Notification** | 1, 5, and 10                    | 3             | No             | Error Log (Delivery Failure)    |
