# Autopay Statuses

| Status   | Description                                                                                 |
|----------|---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------|
| APPROVED | Indicates an approved autopay status.                                                       |
| REJECTED | Indicates a rejected autopay status. It may be related to different reason codes.           |
| FAILED   | Indicates a failed autopay status. It may be related to different reason codes.             |

---

# Reason Codes for Autopay

The following section describes some of the possible response codes that the autopay system may return.
These codes help to understand the status of an operation and allow for clear identification of potential errors or special conditions.

<Note type="warning">
The codes listed in this section do not represent the full set of codes that the system may return.

The integrating merchant must consider that additional codes may be received, including those defined in
the general [**Response Codes**](/en/autopay/codes) section. Therefore, it is recommended to implement flexible response handling
and not restrict the integration exclusively to the codes described here.
</Note>


| Reason Code | Description                                 |
|-------------|---------------------------------------------|
| 00          | Indicates an approved or successful process. |
| ?C          | Indicates a canceled process.               |
| AP          | Indicates a completed payment process.      |

# Autopay Status Transitions

This section describes the scenarios in which an **autopay** can change its status.

| Scenario                                                                                                                                | Status   | Reason |
|-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------|----------|--------|
| Autopay creation.                                                                                                                       | APPROVED | 00     |
| Autopay cancellation.                                                                                                                   | REJECTED | ?C     |
| Autopay completed.                                                                                                                      | REJECTED | AP     |
| The allowed limit of overdue payments is reached.                                                                                       | FAILED   | ?C     |
| The retry limit is reached due to external service unavailability when checking balance during payment registration.                    | FAILED   | ?C     |
| The retry limit is reached due to a technical failure, either during the registration or processing of an autopay installment.          | FAILED   | ?C     |
