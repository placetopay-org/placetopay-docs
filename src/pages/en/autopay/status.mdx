# Autopay Statuses

| Status   | Description                                                                  |
|----------|------------------------------------------------------------------------------|
| APPROVED | Indicates an approved autopay                                                |
| REJECTED | Indicates a rejected autopay and may be related to different reason code's.  |
| FAILED   | Indicates a failed autopay and may be related to different reason code's.    |

---

# Reason Code's that an Autopay may have

| Reason Code | Description                      |
|-------------|----------------------------------|
| 00          | Indicates an approved autopay    |
| ?C          | Indicates a canceled autopay     |
| AP          | Indicates a completed autopay    |

---

# Autopay Status Change by Scenario

| Scenario                                                                                                                             | Status   | Reason |
|--------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------|----------|--------|
| Autopay creation                                                                                                                     | APPROVED | 00     |
| Autopay cancellation                                                                                                                 | REJECTED | ?C     |
| Completed autopay. The last transaction of the autopay is approved.                                                                  | REJECTED | AP     |
| The limit of delayed payments is reached.                                                                                            | FAILED   | ?C     |
| The retry limit is reached due to unavailability of the external service during the payment registration balance inquiry.            | FAILED   | ?C     |
| The retry limit is reached due to technical failure, either during the registration or processing of an autopay installment.         | FAILED   | ?C     |
