# Hold or release payment orders

This endpoint allows you to **hold** or **release** a payment order.
- When a payment order is **held**, its payment status appears as **"in process"** in the admin panel and it **cannot be edited or deleted**.
- To release it, the field `revoke: true` must be used.
- The operation requires the **unique ID of the payment order**, generated by the system at the time of its creation.
- You can send one or multiple payment orders.
